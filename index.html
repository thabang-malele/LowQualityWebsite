About Us

Built From Less.

LowQuality is a black-and-white clothing brand built around the idea that simple does not mean empty.


The name is meant to be ironic. The goal is to make apparel that feels raw, authentic, intentional, and easy to wear. No loud colours or unnecessary noise. Just pieces that fit into everyday life and still carry identity.


Inspired by retro tech, streetwear, minimal design, and digital culture, LowQuality turns basic clothing into something sharper.
